Anthony Edwards leading NBA in made 3-pointers this season

By Will McLernon

Anthony Edwards is leading the NBA in made 3-pointers this according to data from NBA.com

The 23-year-old Minnesota Timberwolves shooting guard has made 158 of 375 attempts from beyond the arc.

Minnesota Timberwolves shooting guard attempts a 3-pointer against the Boston Celtics. (Brian Babineau/NBAE via Getty Images)

The NBA.com data shows Edwards holds the NBA lead for made three-pointers by 13 buckets. Here is the top of the leaderboard for made threes this season:

  1. Anthony Edwards (158)
  2. Malik Beasely & Tyler Herro (145)
  3. Donovan Mitchell(135)
  4. Jayson Tatum (134)
